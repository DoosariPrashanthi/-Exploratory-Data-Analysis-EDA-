# Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

## Dataset
Mobile Price Dataset

## Objective
To understand the dataset using statistics and visualizations.

## Summary Statistics
- Average price: ~441 USD
- Most phones fall between 200–600 USD
- RAM ranges from 4GB to 8GB

## Visualizations Used
- Histogram
- Boxplot
- Correlation Heatmap
- Pairplot

## Observations
- Higher RAM → higher price
- Better camera → slightly higher price
- Most devices have mid-range specs

## Conclusion
EDA shows strong relationships between price and features like RAM and camera.
